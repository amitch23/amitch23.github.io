Andrea graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in Interdisciplinary Field Studies combining Psychology, Sociology, and Visual Studies, with a minor in music. She then worked as a photography assistant in the desert of Utah before moving to Spain and working as an English-Language Assistant in the public school system. Andrea became a certified ESL teacher in 2012 and taught English at the University of Cádiz, while simultaneously teaching at a private language academy.

In the meantime, she taught herself graphic design and web design and worked for a web designer in Los Angeles. While in Spain, Andrea designed and developed websites for local companies.

In her free time, Andrea enjoys songwriting, taking photos, writing and practicing her natural language skills.
